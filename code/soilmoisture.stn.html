Soil moisture percentile time series from 19980101-20180426

Soil moisture percentile are calculated using weibull plotting rank based on 20 years historical data (1998-2017). Blue lines show soil moisture percentile time series from VIC model data, and red lines show soil moisture time series extended from NRCS, SCAN, USCRN and WRCC station observations using MOVE2 method. Soil moisture percentile after 2018 are obtained by looking up the same historical values percentile in 1998-2017.
